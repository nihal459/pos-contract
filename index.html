Point of Sale (POS) and Inventory System

A Comprehensive Tool to Streamline Business Operations

1. Introduction

Bilberry Foods, a distribution company based in Doha, Qatar, utilizes a comprehensive Point of Sale (POS) and Inventory System designed to streamline the day-to-day operations of its retail business. This system automates key processes such as inventory management, sales tracking, purchase orders, expense monitoring, and sales team management. By centralizing these tasks, the POS and Inventory System enhances operational efficiency, ensuring smooth business operations and real-time insights that enable better decision-making for the food industry.

2. Features and Functionalities

Invoice Management
  • Create Invoices with customer details and product listings.
  • Apply discounts accordingly.
  • Print invoices to customers(Shops).
  • Track invoice statuses (paid, unpaid, partially paid).
Product and Category Management
  • Add/edit products with details like SKU, price, and category.
  • Track stock levels with low-stock alerts.
  • Manage categories and assign products accordingly.
Purchase Management
  • Create and manage purchase orders for restocking.
  • Store supplier details and track orders.
Detailed Reporting
  • Analyze performance with sales, inventory, and financial reports.
  • Create custom reports based on business requirements.

Credit and Partial Sales
  • Record sales on credit and track payments.
  • Accept partial payments and update invoice statuses.
Shop Management
  • Manage multiple shops with independent inventories.
  • Configure shop settings, working hours, and offers.
  • Shop Overview: Dashboard for sales, stock levels, and employee performance per shop
Expense Tracker
  • Track expenses by category and manage recurring expenses.
  • Generate detailed monthly and yearly reports.
  • Staff Salary: Generate salary reports based on working hours, leaves, and performance
Salesman Management
  • Set and monitor individual salesmen.
Bulk Product Upload
  • CSV Upload: Upload bulk products through CSV or Excel files.
  • Error Handling: Validate CSV files before upload and provide error reports for corrections.
Analytics and Insights
  • Sales Insights: Generate predictive sales reports and identify trends.
  • Customer Analytics: Track customer preferences, purchase behavior, and frequency.
  • Product Performance: Analyze product-wise performance to optimize inventory and pricing
  • Branch Performance: Compare performance across multiple shop locations




Database Backup
  • The admin has to backup the database regularly and store it in google drive or their personal servers so that if in case the application or pc crashes, the datas and application can be restarted again .
Vehicle Management
  • Manage vehicles that the company has so that it can be assigned to salesmen during an invoice/order generation
